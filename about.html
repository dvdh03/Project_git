About Us

Founded in 1924 by Adi Dassler, Adidas has grown into one of the world’s most iconic sports brands. Headquartered in Herzogenaurach, Germany, we are renowned for our innovative sports apparel, footwear, and accessories. Our mission is to be the global leader in the sporting goods industry, with a relentless commitment to performance and passion for sports. Our signature three-stripe design has become synonymous with quality and athleticism across the globe.


At Adidas, we are driven by a passion for sport, innovation, and pushing boundaries. As one of the world’s leading sportswear brands, we combine high-performance athletic apparel with cutting-edge design. Whether you’re a top athlete or a style enthusiast, our mission is to inspire through our products, making every experience with Adidas a step towards excellence. Rooted in our heritage but always looking ahead, we aim to create solutions that shape the future of sport and fashion, while promoting sustainability and inclusivity.
